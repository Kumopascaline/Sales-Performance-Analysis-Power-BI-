
# Sales Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project features an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze the financial performance of Pamgolet from September 2013 through December 2014. The analysis focuses on identifying seasonal trends, revenue drivers by product, and high-level profitability metrics to support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Insights

* **Annual Growth:** Gross sales significantly increased year-over-year, with 2014 accounting for **77.67%** ($99.37M) of total revenue compared to 2013's **22.33%** ($28.56M).
* **Product Performance:** **Paseo** is the top-performing product by revenue, generating **$33M**, followed by VTT at $21M.
* **Monthly Highlights (December 2014):**
* **Total Profit:** $2.72M.
* **Units Sold:** 155.31K.
* **Total Sales:** $17.37M.
* **Quarterly Trends:** **Q4** recorded the highest profit at **$7.5M**, indicating strong end-of-year performance.


## Visualizations Included

* **Time-Series Analysis:** Line charts tracking "Total Sales Per Month," "Total Units Sold Each Month," and "Profit Over the Year" to visualize volatility and growth.
* **Revenue Segmentation:** A bar chart ranking revenue by product (Paseo, VTT, Velo, etc.).
* **Market Share:** A pie chart illustrating the distribution of gross sales between 2013 and 2014.
* **Profit Metrics:** A quarterly breakdown of profit to identify the most lucrative business periods.
